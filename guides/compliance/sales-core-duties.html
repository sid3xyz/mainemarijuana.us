Sales to Consumers: Core Duties

Non‑negotiable checkpoints for every transaction. Miss one and the sale stops.

Age & ID Verification

  • Verify 21+ before engagement: Ask for ID prior to discussing product or ringing a sale.MRS Title 28-B, §504(4)(A)
  • Acceptable ID only: Government-issued photo ID or options approved by rule; no expired documents.MRS Title 28-B, §504(4)(B)

Purchase & Possession Limits

  • Total weight ≤ 2.5 oz: Combine flower, concentrates, infused products, and accessories that contain cannabis.MRS Title 28-B, §504(2)(B)
  • Concentrate ≤ 10 g: Cartridges, dabs, oils, etc. share this cap, even if total weight is low.MRS Title 28-B, §1501(1)(B)

No‑Go Sales

  • Visibly intoxicated: Decline the sale, document, notify leadership.MRS Title 28-B, §504(2)(D)
  • Prohibited items: No mature plants, and no cannabis products combined with tobacco or alcohol.MRS Title 28-B, §504(2)(A)
  • Human-operated only: No vending or unattended dispensing machines.MRS Title 28-B, §504(2)(C)(1)
