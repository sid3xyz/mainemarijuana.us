Product Packaging & Labeling

Confirm packaging and labels during intake and before handoff to the customer.

Packaging Mandates

  • Child-resistant & tamper-evident: Prepackaged or placed into compliant exit packaging before the sale completes.MRS Title 28-B, §701(2)(A)
  • Measured liquids: Multi-serving liquids must include an integral measuring component for single doses.MRS Title 28-B, §701(2)(C)

Label Requirements

  • Mandatory fields: License numbers (cultivator, manufacturer, store), universal symbol, warnings, batch, net weight, cannabinoid potency.MRS Title 28-B, §701(1)
  • Prohibited content: No youth-oriented graphics, false claims, or trademark violations; avoid depicting humans/animals/fruit.MRS Title 28-B, §701(4)

Edible Standards

  • Potency caps: ≤ 10 mg THC per serving, ≤ 200 mg per package.MRS Title 28-B, §703(1)(F)
  • Homogeneous dosing: Cannabinoids distributed evenly across the product.MRS Title 28-B, §703(1)(B)
  • Shape rules: No shapes resembling humans, animals, or fruit.MRS Title 28-B, §703(1)(E)
