Opening routine

Opening Briefing

  • • Review compliance bulletins and overnight incident notes.
  • • Assign roles (greeter, inventory runner, closing lead) before doors open.
  • • Set two measurable goals for the shift and log outcomes.
During shift

Performance Tracking

  • • Monitor attach rates, loyalty capture, and queue times each hour.
  • • Coach in the moment—celebrate wins and reset scripts immediately.
  • • Record notable performance trends for weekly one-on-ones.
Escalations

Conflict Management

  • • Use LEAP (Listen, Empathize, Apologize, Provide next steps) for guest issues.
  • • Document disruptive behavior and trespass warnings with time stamps.
  • • Follow HR escalation flow for staff disputes and recap outcomes.

Daily

Daily Compliance Check

  • • Verify surveillance uptime, safe counts, and refuse log entries.
  • • Ensure all required signage and licenses are posted and undamaged.
  • • Capture findings in the compliance tracker with photos where needed.
Inventory

METRC Reconciliation

  • • Compare physical counts to METRC packages before close.
  • • Investigate variances immediately—no carryover discrepancies.
  • • File discrepancy forms and notify the compliance lead.
People

Staff Certification

  • • Maintain a roster of training completion dates and upcoming renewals.
  • • Pair new hires with mentors and document shadow shifts.
  • • Escalate missed deadlines or policy failures to HR within 24 hours.
Readiness

OCP Inspection Prep

  • • Keep digital and physical binders synchronized weekly.
  • • Update response scripts for common inspector questions.
  • • Stage mock inspections quarterly and document improvement items.
Security

Security Protocols

  • • Audit key fob permissions and deactivate former employees immediately.
  • • Confirm panic buttons, alarms, and CCTV backup storage.
  • • Log any incident or police interaction with supporting evidence.

Analytics

Sales Reporting

  • • Automate daily summaries with gross/net, markdowns, and tax detail.
  • • Highlight anomalies (void spikes, low conversion) for leadership review.
  • • Archive reports for at least two years in shared storage.
Guest voice

Customer Insights

  • • Track NPS, review scores, and direct feedback weekly.
  • • Tag issues (wait time, product availability, service) for trend spotting.
  • • Loop wins into coaching moments with shout-outs.
Compliance

Regulatory Reporting

  • • Maintain a compliance calendar with state, municipal, and tax deadlines.
  • • Double-check filings against METRC data and financial systems.
  • • Store submission receipts and confirmation emails centrally.

Log escalations, METRC variances, and training actions in a shared tracker so relief managers can pick up instantly.
