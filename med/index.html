Your Guide to the Maine Medical Cannabis Program

Navigating the path to medical cannabis in Maine, from doctor consultation to dispensary visit.

How It Works: The Path for Patients

Maine's medical program is based on a doctor's recommendation. There is no specific list of qualifying conditions.

1

Talk to Your Doctor

Any licensed Maine physician can certify you for medical cannabis if they believe it will be beneficial for your condition.

2

Get Your Certification

Your doctor will provide you with a medical certification. This is your official document for the program.

3

Visit a Dispensary or Caregiver

With your certification and a valid ID, you can purchase from any licensed medical dispensary or registered caregiver in Maine.
