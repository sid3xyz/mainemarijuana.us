Medical Cannabis Purchase Limits

A qualifying patient may purchase up to:

2.5 Ounces

(70.87 grams) of cannabis or cannabis products in a 15-day period.

Source: Title 22, §2423-A

Important

The total weight of all products, including edibles, tinctures, and topicals, counts toward this limit. Always verify patient status and purchase history.

This notice must be posted in a conspicuous location at all retail sales areas.

Print this sign and display it prominently at all point-of-sale stations. Use the print button above or press Ctrl+P / Cmd+P.
