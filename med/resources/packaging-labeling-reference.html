Packaging & Labeling Verification Checklist

Use this checklist when receiving new products to verify compliance with Maine's packaging and labeling requirements. All products must meet these standards before being offered for sale.

Do not sell products that fail these checks. Any packaging or labeling deficiencies must be:

  1. Quarantined in secure storage
  2. Documented with specific compliance issues
  3. Returned to the distributor or repackaged if regulations allow

Keep this checklist with receiving documentation for quick compliance verification.
