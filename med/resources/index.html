Medical Program Required Signage and Staff References

Compliance materials for Maine Medical Use of Cannabis Program (MMCP) dispensaries, caregivers, and cultivation facilities. All items print on letter-size paper with consistent formatting.

Based on Maine Medical Use of Cannabis Act (Title 22 MRS Chapter 558-C)

  • Qualifying patient possession limits: 2.5 oz usable cannabis (dispensary/caregiver transaction limit), 8 lbs harvested cannabis (home cultivation)
  • Cultivation rights: 6 mature plants, 12 immature plants, unlimited seedlings per patient
  • Registered caregiver limits: No patient limit (cultivation limits: 30 mature/60 immature plants OR 500/1000 sq ft canopy)
  • Unregistered family/household caregivers: 2 patients maximum (must be family/household members)
  • All materials cite current Maine Revised Statutes (Title 22 MRS §2423-A, §2428 - verified October 2025)
  • Sources: Maine OCP Medical Use Program, 22 MRS §2423-A
