Patient Rights & Responsibilities

Understanding the rules of the Maine Medical Use of Marijuana Program.

Possession & Purchase Limits

As a qualifying patient, you are legally permitted to purchase and possess medical cannabis within specific limits.

The 2.5-Ounce Limit

You may purchase up to 2.5 ounces (70.87 grams) of cannabis and cannabis products in a 15-day period. This is an aggregate limit, meaning the total weight of all your products (flower, edibles, tinctures, etc.) counts toward the total. Me. Rev. Stat. Tit. 22, §2423-D

Home Cultivation

The program allows you to cultivate your own medicine under specific guidelines.

The Six-Plant Limit

You or your designated primary caregiver may cultivate up to six mature cannabis plants. This allows for a personal supply of medicine, but any cultivation must be in a private, secure location.

Visiting Maine as a Medical Patient

Maine allows patients from other states to purchase medical cannabis, provided their state is on the approved list.

Reciprocity

If you are a registered medical cannabis patient in another state, you may be able to purchase medicine in Maine. You must present your valid patient credentials from your home state. No separate Maine certification is required. For a full list of approved states, please consult the official OCP website.
