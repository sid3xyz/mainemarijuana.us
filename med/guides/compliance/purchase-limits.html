Purchase Limits in Practice

Use these examples to confirm weight calculations and identify red flags quickly.

ScenarioProductsOutcomeKey Note
Mixed Flower + Cartridges 1 oz flower; four 1 g cartridges Legal Total 32.35 g (under 70.87 g); concentrate only 4 g.
Max Concentrate + Flower 10 g concentrate; 2 oz flower Legal Concentrate cap reached; total 66.7 g stays under limit.
Edibles + Flower 3.5 g flower; five 50 g bars (100 mg each) Illegal Entire edible net weight counts (253.5 g) > 70.87 g limit. 28‑B §1501(1)(B); OCP Edible Weight Guidance (2021)
Cartridge Stack Eleven 1 g cartridges Illegal Exceeds 10 g concentrate cap even though total weight is low. 28‑B §1501(1)(B); OCP Concentrate Cap Guidance (2024)
Full Flower + Extra 2.5 oz flower; 1 g live resin Illegal Flower alone meets total limit; nothing else allowed. 28‑B §1501(1)(B); OCP Implementation Update (2024)

Tip: Log borderline orders in the shift handoff so the next team can watch for patterns.
