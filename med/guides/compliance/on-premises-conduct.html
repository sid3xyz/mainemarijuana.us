On‑Premises Conduct

Maintain compliance inside the store as diligently as during sales.

  • No on‑site consumption: Customers may not consume on the licensed premises.MRS Title 22, §508(3)(B)
  • No on‑site consumption: Customers may not consume on the licensed premises.MRS Title 22, §508(3)(B); OCP Delivery & Curbside Guidance (2022)
  • Minor access: Minors only allowed when accompanied by a parent, legal guardian, or custodian.MRS Title 22, §507
  • Minor access: Minors only allowed when accompanied by a parent, legal guardian, or custodian.MRS Title 22, §507; OCP Hospitality Task Force (2023)
  • License display: Post the current license conspicuously at all times.MRS Title 22, §509
  • License display: Post the current license conspicuously at all times.MRS Title 22, §509; OCP Outdoor Cultivation Guidance (2022)
