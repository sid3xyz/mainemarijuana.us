Customer FAQs After Purchase

Keep responses short and cite the rule when possible.

  • Where to consume: Private residence or property with explicit permission; public consumption remains prohibited.MRS Title 22, §1501(2)
  • Home grow limits: Up to 6 mature and 12 immature plants per adult for personal use.MRS Title 22, §1502(1)
  • Home grow safeguards: Plants can’t be visible from public ways without optical aid; secure against access by anyone under 21.MRS Title 22, §1502(2)
