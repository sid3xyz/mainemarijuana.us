Verification & Important Information

This directory contains retailers licensed for adult-use (recreational) cannabis sales in Maine. Always verify current licensing status before visiting.

For the most current and complete list of licensed retailers, visit the Maine Office of Cannabis Policy website. License status can change, and this directory may not reflect real-time updates.

  • All customers must be 21 years or older with valid ID
  • Purchase limits: 2.5 oz flower aggregate, 10g concentrate sub-limit
  • Cash transactions only at most locations
  • Products cannot be consumed on-site or in public
