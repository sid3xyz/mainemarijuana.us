Adult-Use Program Signage and Staff References

All items below open dedicated print pages with consistent margins, typography, and usage guidance.

Maine statute mandates ONLY ONE facility posting requirement:

  • License Display (Title 28-B §509): Your active cannabis license must be conspicuously displayed within the licensed premises at all times. This is the ONLY legally required posting.

All other signage below is RECOMMENDED as best practice for operational compliance and customer transparency but is NOT explicitly mandated by Maine statute. Citations refer to the underlying legal requirements that these signs help communicate (e.g., age verification PRACTICES under §504(4), not signage requirements; §701 addresses PRODUCT labeling, not facility signage).

Verified against current Maine statute (data extracted 1/07/2025). Sources: Title 28-B §509, §504, §508, §701
