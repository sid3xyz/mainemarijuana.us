Daily Management Checklist

Start every shift with these compliance-critical steps. Print for handoff.

  • Review compliance bulletins and overnight incident notes. OCP Guidance
  • Assign roles (greeter, inventory runner, closing lead) before doors open.
  • Verify signage, ID equipment, and product labeling. 28-B §509, §504(4), §701
  • Conduct opening inventory reconciliation in METRC. 28-B §105
  • Confirm staff training status and compliance knowledge.

Compliance Oversight

Manager duties for regulatory compliance and operational integrity.

  • Monitor all transactions for compliance with purchase limits, ID checks, and prohibited sales. 28-B §504, §1501
  • Oversee daily METRC reconciliation and variance reporting. 28-B §105
  • Ensure all corrective actions and compliance reports are documented and retained for 2 years. 28-B §305
  • Respond to OCP bulletins and update SOPs as needed.

Incident Escalation Protocols

Follow these steps for common incidents. Document and escalate per SOP.

  • Failed ID scan or suspected fake: Escalate to management, log incident, and notify OCP if unresolved. 28-B §504(4)
  • Suspected diversion, theft, or security breach: Document, notify ownership, and report to OCP. 28-B §303
  • Inventory discrepancy: Attempt reconciliation, escalate unresolved issues to OCP. 28-B §105
  • Customer dispute or compliance challenge: Remain calm, cite the rule, and offer to escalate to OCP or legal counsel.

Staff Training Procedures

Ensure all staff are trained on compliance, METRC workflows, and incident handling.

  • Conduct onboarding and refresher training for all staff. OCP Guidance
  • Monitor staff performance and provide corrective coaching as needed.
  • Train staff on METRC workflows, error resolution, and compliance protocols. 28-B §105
  • Document all training sessions and retain records for audit. 28-B §305

Audit Preparation

Prepare for OCP inspections and regulatory audits.

  • Prepare all METRC records, logs, and manifests for inspection. 28-B §301
  • Conduct mock audits to ensure readiness. OCP Guidance
  • Ensure sales, inventory, and transfer logs are accessible within minutes.
  • Retain all audit documentation for 2 years. 28-B §305

Daily

Daily Compliance Check

  • • Verify surveillance uptime, safe counts, and refuse log entries.
  • • Ensure all required signage and licenses are posted and undamaged.
  • • Capture findings in the compliance tracker with photos where needed.
Inventory

METRC Reconciliation

  • • Compare physical counts to METRC packages before close.
  • • Investigate variances immediately—no carryover discrepancies.
  • • File discrepancy forms and notify the compliance lead.
People

Staff Certification

  • • Maintain a roster of training completion dates and upcoming renewals.
  • • Pair new hires with mentors and document shadow shifts.
  • • Escalate missed deadlines or policy failures to HR within 24 hours.
Readiness

OCP Inspection Prep

  • • Keep digital and physical binders synchronized weekly.
  • • Update response scripts for common inspector questions.
  • • Stage mock inspections quarterly and document improvement items.
Security

Security Protocols

  • • Audit key fob permissions and deactivate former employees immediately.
  • • Confirm panic buttons, alarms, and CCTV backup storage.
  • • Log any incident or police interaction with supporting evidence.
