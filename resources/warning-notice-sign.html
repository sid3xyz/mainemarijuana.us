Cannabis Product Warning Notice

Every cannabis retail storefront must display the following warnings in a location that is clearly visible to customers. Post near the point of sale and keep signage legible at all times.

MRS Title 28-B, §701(4)

Required warnings

Health warning

  • This product has intoxicating effects and may be habit forming.
  • Smoking cannabis can be hazardous to your health.
  • There may be health risks associated with consumption of this product.
  • Should not be used by women who are pregnant or breastfeeding.
  • For use only by adults 21 years of age or older. Keep out of the reach of children.
  • Cannabis can impair concentration, coordination, and judgment. Do not operate a vehicle or machinery under the influence of this drug.

State law prohibits

  • Use of cannabis products in public places
  • Use of cannabis products on federal property or lands
  • Transportation of cannabis products across state lines
  • Distribution or sale to individuals under 21 years of age
  • Possession of more than 2.5 ounces of cannabis or 10 grams of concentrate

Delayed effects advisory

For edible products: The intoxicating effects of edible cannabis may be delayed by 2 or more hours. Start with a small portion and wait at least 2 hours before consuming more.

For inhaled products: Effects may be felt immediately but can intensify over time. Start with a small amount and wait before using more.

If someone is experiencing adverse effects from cannabis consumption:

  • Call Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222
  • For medical emergencies call 911

Display this notice in a conspicuous location visible to all customers.

Print and post this notice near customer entry or checkout. Use the print button above or press Ctrl+P / Cmd+P.
