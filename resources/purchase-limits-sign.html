Legal Cannabis Purchase & Possession Limits

Maine law sets strict limits on the amount of cannabis and cannabis products a retail store can sell to an adult customer in a single transaction or day.

28‑B §504(2)(B); §1501(1)(B), (F)

Transaction limits per customer

Total cannabis limit

2.5 oz
70.87 grams total weight

Combined weight of all cannabis forms

Concentrate sub-limit

10 grams
Counts toward the 2.5 oz total

Includes vape cartridges, wax, shatter, etc.

Purchase limits: Up to 2.5 oz of cannabis or a combination that includes no more than 10 g of concentrate per 24 hours. 28‑B §1501(1)(B), (F)

For edibles and infused beverages, count the entire net product weight toward the 2.5 oz aggregate. For the 10 g concentrate sub‑limit, count only the weight of cannabis concentrate in a product (exclude other ingredients) per definitions. 28‑B §1501(1)(B), (F); §102‑A(9)

Edible product THC limits

Single serving

10 mg THC

Maximum THC per serving 28‑B §703(1)(F)

Total package

200 mg THC

Maximum THC per package 28‑B §703(1)(F)

Common transaction scenarios

Example Products Outcome Reason
Mixed products 1 oz flower + four 1 g vape cartridges Legal Total is 32.35 g with 4 g concentrate (under both limits)
Concentrate focus 10 g concentrates + 2 oz flower Legal At concentrate limit but total (66.7 g) under main limit
Heavy edibles 3.5 g flower + five 50 g edibles Illegal Total weight (253.5 g) exceeds 70.87 g limit
Concentrate excess Eleven 1 g vape cartridges Illegal Exceeds 10 g concentrate limit
